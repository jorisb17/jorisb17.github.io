“A conscience robot employee Hestia notices suspicious behavior from the facility AI Kronos causing her to decide to sabotage the factory with her access to tools and stop the AI in charge, which turns out to be controlled by the creator of the factory in an attempt to proof his worth to his family.”

Malfunction is a puzzle adventure game with a nice flare of mystery. To write a good story with a good bit of conflict we aim it at 12 years and older. This way we can make it pretty spooky without being childish.

In order to make the game feel full, polished and complex, we will keep it small and focus on 3 levels to start with. Filling it we will use only in-house made 3D assets. These assets together will create awesome and intrinsic puzzles.

During the game we want to make it more spooky and scary as you get closer to the end boss and for that we will create immersive music. Our character will be controlled from a third-person perspective in order to let the player easily see what items their character is using but still keeping the player close by and in the game.

About

Welcome to Chrono CORP. The place to find the best household robots on the planet. We are also the only place that sells them. Our entire civilization has become dependent on our product so you can imagine the importance of a smooth production process.

My name is Jeff and I am the creator of Chrono CORP. To make sure everything runs smoothly we have created a workforce that only consists out of robots. It is a fact that robots make less mistakes than humans and more importantly you don’t have to pay them. So more profit for me.

Kronos is our Artificial Intelligence specifically created for this factory. Every robot in this factory is connected to Kronos. Kronos is responsible for handing out the tasks and to oversee that every task is completed in time. You will be joining our factory as our newest repair robot Hestia.

You will replace you predecessor as that robot made one mistake to many and got a rapid disassembly. As one of our repair robots it’s your responsibility to fix the broken machines. To do this you need special tools that you can find everywhere on the work floor. Sadly we have not made a general storage area for tools. Every machine requires a different tool. To reach some of the machines you will need to think beyond the box. But with your critical thinking capabilities I do not think those little puzzles will be a challenge.

If your nervous don’t be. Remember you are a robot and Kronos will be there to guide you through your fist workday. Oh and if you see any weird behavior, report it immediately to Kronos. This also includes my brother Kevin

Gameplay

  • Switch arms.

    Hestia is a technician. So, she must repair machines. But she cannot do it with her "normal arms". Therefor she must switch arms to do her job. Find the right arms to improve your work and find around your tasks to help the factory!

  • Become an expert in puzzles.

    Join Hestia to repair machines and walk around the factory. But it is not just repairing and walking through the factory. By repairing machines, you must solve puzzles. But you can also dissolve puzzles to break things. In addition, the factory is also a puzzle, you must puzzle your way to your destination. So, puzzle to your own advantage!

  • Be a good sneaker.

    Because the employees malfunction, it may be wise to leave your colleagues alone before something bad happens. Scan through the factory to see where it is best to walk. And do not get spotted. Because if you are spotted by someone, you might get caught. And maybe terrible things can happen that you do not want.
